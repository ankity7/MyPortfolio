About Me

My name is Ankit Yadav. I am currently pursuing a PhD in Astroparticle Physics at Khalifa University, under the supervision of Dr. Satyendra Thoudam. I joined KU in August 2022. Prior to this, I completed my BSMS from the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER Mohali) with a Major in Physics and a Specialization in Astrophysics. My Master's thesis focused on constraining the cosmological constant for dark energy models using Pantheon SN data. My broad interests include Astroparticle Physics, with a focus on Galactic and Extragalactic cosmic rays, cosmology, and large-scale structures.

My Teachings

I have been involved in teaching various undergraduate courses, including Classical Mechanics, Electrodynamics, and Introduction to Astrophysics. My approach to teaching emphasizes a deep understanding of fundamental concepts, coupled with practical problem-solving skills. I have also supervised several student projects in related fields.

Science Activities

As an active member of the scientific community, I participate in numerous conferences, workshops, and seminars. I have presented my research at several international conferences and am involved in collaborative projects with researchers from different institutions. My activities also include public outreach programs aimed at promoting science education and awareness.
